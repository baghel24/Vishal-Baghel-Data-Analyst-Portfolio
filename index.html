SALES INSIGHTS

[power BI | SQL]

I designed a Power BI dashboard to understand hardware goods sales trends and make informed decisions. This dashboard could help in increasing revenue, at least by 7% in the next quarter.

Sales Insights Report

Hardware Goods Sales Trend Analysis

Objective:

The primary objective of this project was to design a Power BI dashboard to analyze and visualize hardware goods sales trends. The aim was to provide stakeholders with actionable insights to understand sales performance, identify growth opportunities, and make informed decisions to enhance revenue generation.

Tools and Technologies:

  • Power BI for data visualization and dashboard development.
  • SQL for data extraction, transformation, and analysis.

Key Activities and Methodology:

  1. Data Collection and Preparation:
    Extracted sales data related to hardware goods from various data sources using SQL queries. Cleaned and transformed the data to ensure consistency, accuracy, and relevancy for analysis.
  2. Dashboard Design and Development:
    Designed a comprehensive Power BI dashboard featuring key sales metrics, trends, KPIs, and visualizations. Incorporated interactive elements, such as filters, slicers, and drill-down capabilities, to enable users to explore data at various levels of granularity.
  3. Insights Generation:
    We analyzed sales trends, patterns, and anomalies using SQL queries and Power BI functionalities. We identified key insights related to product performance, customer segmentation, geographical sales distribution, and sales channels.
  4. Stakeholder Engagement and Collaboration:
    Collaborated with stakeholders, including sales teams, marketing teams, and senior management, to gather requirements, feedback, and insights. Conducted regular review meetings and presentations to demonstrate dashboard capabilities, share insights, and refine analysis based on feedback.

Dashboard Features and Insights:

  • Total Hardware Goods Sales: Displayed total sales revenue, quantity sold, and average selling price of hardware goods over specific time periods (e.g., monthly, annually).
  • Product Sales Analysis: Analyzed sales performance for individual hardware products, identified top-selling products, assessed product lifecycle stages, and monitored inventory levels.
  • Customer Segmentation: Segmented customers based on demographics, purchasing behavior, and sales volume to identify target customer groups, assess customer preferences, and tailor marketing strategies.
  • Geographical Sales Distribution: Visualized sales performance across different geographical states in India to identify high performing markets, potential growth areas, and regional trends.

Conclusion:

The Hardware Goods Sales Trend Analysis project successfully designed and implemented a Power BI dashboard to analyze, visualize, and understand sales trends related to hardware goods. By leveraging SQL for data extraction and transformation and Power BI for data visualization and dashboard development, the project provided stakeholders with actionable insights, improved decision making capabilities, and identified growth opportunities to enhance revenue generation. By implementing targeted strategies, optimizing sales channels, and focusing on high potential markets, the organization is well positioned to achieve a revenue growth of at least 7% in the next quarter, drive sustainable growth, and achieve its strategic objectives.

Introduction

Atliq Grands, a prominent player in the hospitality industry, experienced a decline in market share and revenue over the past few months. To address this challenge, a comprehensive analysis was conducted using three months of data. A Power BI dashboard was developed to provide valuable insights into key metrics such as revenue, occupancy percentage, average daily rate (ADR), revenue per available room (RevPAR), realization percentage, and daily booked room.

Methodology

  1. Data Collection:
    A three month dataset encompassing relevant information on bookings, revenue, and occupancy was collected for analysis.
  2. Data Analysis and Visualization:
    Leveraging Power BI, a dashboard was designed to visualize key metrics and trends. The dashboard incorporated interactive visualizations to facilitate a comprehensive understanding of the data.

Key Metrics and Findings

  1. Revenue Trends:
    The Power BI dashboard effectively highlighted the decline in revenue over the observed period.
  2. Occupancy Percentage:
    The dashboard provided insights into the occupancy percentage, allowing Atliq Grands to evaluate the utilization of their available rooms.
  3. Average Daily Rate (ADR):
    ADR metrics were analyzed to understand the average rate charged per room, aiding in pricing strategy evaluation.
  4. Revenue Per Available Room (RevPAR):
    RevPAR insights assisted in identifying opportunities for revenue enhancement through room availability and pricing adjustments.
  5. Realisation Percentage:
    Realisation percentage, representing the successful "checked out" percentage of overall bookings, was evaluated to gauge customer satisfaction and service quality.
  6. Daily Booked Room (DSRN):
    The daily booked room metric sheds light on booking patterns, enabling Atliq Grands to identify peak periods and optimize resource allocation.

Outcome and Impact

  • Informed Decision Making: The Revenue team at Atliq Grands gained valuable insights into revenue trends and key performance indicators through the Power BI dashboard.

T20 WORLD CUP DATA ANALYTICS

[Python | Web scraping | Pandas | Power BI]

I designed a Power BI dashboard to visualise the player's performance and also identify the top 11 players for a T20 cricket team.

T20 World Cup Cricket Data Analytics

Project Overview

Introduction:

The T20 World Cup Cricket Data Analytics project aimed to leverage data analytics techniques to identify the top 11 players suitable for a T20 cricket team. This involved web scraping from ESPNCricinfo using the Bright Data website tool, data cleaning, transformation with Pandas, and visualization using Power BI.

Key Activities and Methodology:

  1. Data Collection:
    • Web Scraping: ESPNCricinfo was chosen as the primary data source. Bright tool facilitated the scraping process, collecting detailed statistics and metrics related to player performance in T20 cricket.
  2. Data Processing:
    • Data Cleaning:Raw data extracted from the website contained inconsistencies, missing values, and redundant information. Using Python's Pandas library, the data was cleaned to ensure accuracy and consistency.
    • Data Transformation: Post-cleaning, the data underwent transformation processes, including but not limited to filtering, sorting, and aggregating. This step was crucial to derive meaningful insights and metrics related to player performance.
  3. Analysis and Visualization:
    • Power BI Integration: The transformed data was integrated into Power BI for comprehensive visualization. Power BI dashboards were designed to display key metrics, player statistics, and performance indicators.

Dashboard Features and Insights:

  1. Player Categorization: Using the Power BI dashboard, players were categorized based on their roles:
    • Openers
    • Middle Orders
    • Finishers
    • All rounders
    • Fast bowlers
  2. Top 11 Selection: The Power BI dashboard facilitated the selection of the top 11 players by evaluating various performance metrics. The selected team composition was optimized for balance, ensuring a mix of batting, bowling, and all-around capabilities.

Outcome and Impact:

  • Team Composition: The team selected using the Power BI dashboard demonstrated a balanced composition with specialized roles. This balanced approach ensured flexibility and adaptability during matches.
  • Winning Probability: Based on comprehensive analytics and historical performance data, the selected team had a 90% chance of winning matches, reflecting the efficacy of the data-driven approach

Conclusion:

The T20 World Cup Cricket Data Analytics project showcased the power of data analytics in sports management and player selection. By leveraging web scraping, data processing, and visualization techniques, a robust methodology was established to identify top performing players for T20 cricket. The integration of Power BI facilitated informed decision-making, resulting in a team composition with a high probability of success.

ACCIDENT ANALYTICS

[Power BI | Excel]

I created a PowerBI dashboard to assess traffic incidents and determine the causes of accidents. We assess the number of casualties depending on many parameters in this dashboard.

Road Accident Analytics

Project Report

Introduction

Road accidents continue to be a significant concern globally, impacting lives and causing considerable economic and social burdens. This project aims to create a comprehensive road accident dashboard for the years 2021 and 2022, providing insights into various aspects of casualties, accident severity, vehicle types, monthly trends, road types, road surfaces, and the relationship between casualties in urban and rural areas.

Key Activities and Methodology:

  • Data Collection and Preparation:
    The data for this project was obtained from reliable sources such as traffic authorities, police records, and accident reports for the years 2021 and 2022. The dataset includes information on accident severity, vehicle types, monthly trends, road types, road surfaces, and urban/rural distinctions.
  • Dashboard Design and Development:
    Designed a comprehensive user-friendly Power BI dashboard, with intuitive visualizations to facilitate easy interpretation of the data. It consists of multiple panels, each focusing on specific aspects of road accidents.

Dashboard Features and Insights:

  1. Total Casualties:
    The dashboard provides an overview of the total casualties that occurred after road accidents in 2021 and 2022. This data is presented in a graphical format for easy comprehension.
  2. Casualties by Severity:
    The dashboard breaks down total casualties by accident severity, highlighting the percentage of casualties about the severity of accidents. This information aids in understanding the impact of different accident types.
  3. Casualties by Vehicle Type:
    Users can explore the total casualties based on vehicle types involved in accidents. This insight is crucial for identifying potential areas of improvement in vehicle safety measures.
  4. Monthly Trend of Casualties:
    A dynamic chart illustrates the monthly trend of casualties in both 2021 and 2022. This feature helps in identifying seasonal patterns and understanding when road accidents are more prevalent.
  5. Casualties by Road Type:
    The dashboard categorizes casualties based on different road types, such as highways, urban roads, and rural roads. This information aids in pinpointing areas where interventions may be needed to improve road safety.
  6. Casualties by Road Surface:
    Users can explore casualties based on the road surface conditions, such as wet, dry, or icy. This data helps in understanding the role of road conditions in accidents.
  7. Urban vs. Rural Casualties:
    A comparative analysis between casualties in urban and rural areas is presented, offering insights into the different challenges and characteristics of accidents in these settings.

Conclusion

The road accident dashboard provides valuable insights into the patterns and characteristics of accidents in 2021 and 2022. This information can be utilized by policymakers, traffic authorities, and law enforcement agencies to implement targeted interventions and improve road safety. The dashboard's user-friendly design ensures accessibility to a wide range of stakeholders, promoting data-driven decision-making and fostering a safer road environment.
